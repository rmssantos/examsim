# AB-730 Study Research

## Credential

- Exam: AB-730
- Certification: Microsoft Certified: AI Business Professional
- Level: Beginner
- Role focus: Business users and administrators who use generative AI productivity tools without building AI apps or writing code.
- Primary products: Microsoft 365 Copilot, Microsoft 365 apps, Copilot Chat, Researcher, Analyst, and Copilot agents.
- Exam duration: 45 minutes
- Passing score: 700 or greater on the official Microsoft certification score scale. The simulator metadata stores this as `passScore: 70` because local scoring is percentage-based.
- Language observed: English

## Audience Profile

Candidates should have experience using generative AI-powered productivity tools, including Microsoft 365 Copilot, Researcher, and Analyst. They should use AI to improve daily work, make informed decisions, and drive business outcomes without writing code or building AI apps. Candidates should understand Microsoft 365 basics and be comfortable with Outlook, Word, Teams, PowerPoint, Excel, document work, presentations, image generation, and common business workflows.

## Official Training Coverage

Course AB-730T00 maps to the learning path "Transform business workflows with generative AI". The path is beginner level, about 4 hr 30 min, and contains six modules:

1. What is generative AI?
2. Employ Copilot Chat as your AI assistant
3. Draft and refine business content using Microsoft 365 Copilot
4. Analyze and visualize data using Microsoft 365 Copilot
5. Manage meetings and collaboration using Microsoft 365 Copilot
6. Define the role you want Microsoft 365 Copilot to play in your business workflow
